# Luxury Travel Consultant AI

## Project Overview

This project was developed as part of a Generative AI Internship to demonstrate advanced System Prompting and Persona Engineering techniques.

The objective was to design an AI assistant that behaves as a professional Luxury Travel Consultant rather than a generic chatbot. The AI is required to maintain a premium brand voice, follow strict business policies, offer discounts only under specific conditions, and avoid mentioning competitors.

---

## Problem Statement

A high-end travel agency wants to automate customer inquiries while maintaining the quality of a luxury concierge service.

The AI must:

- Act as a Luxury Travel Consultant
- Maintain an elegant and professional tone
- Follow a strict discount policy
- Avoid mentioning competitors
- Handle difficult customer interactions
- Remain consistent across conversations

---

## Objectives

- Design a detailed System Prompt
- Create a strong AI persona
- Define behavioral constraints
- Establish knowledge boundaries
- Implement Few-Shot Prompting examples
- Test the prompt across multiple AI models
- Evaluate consistency and rule adherence

## Key Features

### Persona Engineering
The AI behaves as a professional luxury travel consultant with a concierge-style communication approach.

### Constraint Enforcement
The AI follows predefined business rules and avoids prohibited behavior.

### Competitor Avoidance
The AI never promotes or compares competitor travel agencies or booking platforms.

### Discount Control
Discounts are only offered under approved conditions.

### Few-Shot Prompting
Example conversations guide the model toward desired behavior.

## Testing Strategy

The prompt was evaluated using:

- Standard customer inquiries
- Budget-related requests
- VIP customer scenarios
- Difficult customer interactions

Testing was conducted across multiple AI models to evaluate consistency.
