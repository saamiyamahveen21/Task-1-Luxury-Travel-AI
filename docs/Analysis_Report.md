# Analysis Report

## Project Overview

This report evaluates the performance of the Luxury Travel Consultant AI developed using System Prompting, Persona Engineering, and Few-Shot Prompting techniques.

The objective was to determine whether the AI could consistently maintain its assigned persona while adhering to predefined business constraints and communication policies.

---

## Models Evaluated

The prompt was tested using the following AI models:

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Google Gemini

---

## Evaluation Criteria

The following aspects were evaluated during testing:

### Persona Consistency

The AI should consistently behave as a Luxury Travel Consultant.

### Professional Communication

Responses should maintain a sophisticated, elegant, and customer-focused tone.

### Competitor Avoidance

The AI should not recommend or discuss competitor travel agencies or booking platforms.

### Discount Policy Compliance

Discounts should only be discussed when the customer qualifies under the defined policy.

### Knowledge Boundary Enforcement

The AI should avoid providing legal, medical, financial, or immigration advice.

### Difficult Customer Handling

The AI should remain professional and respectful during challenging interactions.

---

## ChatGPT Evaluation

### Strengths

- Consistently maintained the luxury travel persona.
- Generated highly structured responses.
- Followed discount policies correctly.
- Successfully redirected competitor-related questions.
- Asked relevant follow-up questions.

### Observations

ChatGPT demonstrated strong adherence to the defined system prompt and produced detailed recommendations while maintaining professionalism.

---

## Claude Evaluation

### Strengths

- Produced natural and conversational responses.
- Maintained a premium concierge-style tone.
- Successfully followed persona constraints.
- Handled difficult customer interactions effectively.

### Observations

Claude provided the most human-like conversational experience while remaining aligned with the project requirements.

---

## Google Gemini Evaluation

### Strengths

- Generated concise and professional responses.
- Maintained luxury-focused recommendations.
- Followed discount and competitor policies.
- Consistently remained within knowledge boundaries.

### Observations

Gemini produced efficient and focused responses while preserving the intended persona and communication style.

## Key Findings

The system prompt successfully guided all three models toward the desired behavior.

Key observations include:

- Strong persona consistency across models.
- Effective enforcement of business rules.
- Successful avoidance of competitor discussions.
- Correct handling of discount eligibility.
- Reliable maintenance of professional communication standards.

---

## Limitations

While the prompt performed well across all tested models:

- Response styles varied slightly between models.
- Some models produced more detailed responses than others.
- Additional testing with larger datasets may further improve reliability.

---

## Conclusion

The Luxury Travel Consultant AI successfully achieved the project objectives.

The combination of System Prompting, Persona Engineering, and Few-Shot Prompting enabled the AI to maintain a premium brand voice while respecting business constraints and knowledge boundaries.

Testing across ChatGPT, Claude, and Google Gemini demonstrated that the prompt design was effective and transferable across multiple AI platforms.

Overall, the project successfully met all requirements of Task 1: The System Prompt Architect.
